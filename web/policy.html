To operate the Quiz24 app, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information: name, mobile number, email address, profile details.

Your information is used strictly for operating and improving Quiz24:

  • To calculate scores, rankings, and rewards.
  • To process wallet transactions and withdrawals.

  • To detect fraud, multiple accounts, or misuse of the platform.
  • To improve app performance, security, and user experience.
