Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 9 Sep 2025

OnlinePaisa ("we", "us", "our") operates a business marketplace app where users can purchase raw materials, manufacture products (such as agarbatti, handmade goods, etc.) and sell the finished products back to us for income. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

To operate the OnlinePaisa platform, we collect the following information:

  • Personal details: name, phone number, email address, address, profile information.
  • Business & transaction data: raw material purchases, product submissions, earnings, delivery details, order history, and payout details.
  • Payment information: UPI ID, bank account details (for payouts). We do not store full debit/credit card details.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, app version, IP address, crash logs, and analytics.
  • Location data: if permission is granted, used for verifying pickup/delivery locations and ensuring service availability.
  • Communications: in‑app messages, support chats, call logs routed via the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to operate and improve the OnlinePaisa income platform:

  • To allow you to buy raw materials and resell finished products through the app.
  • To track your work orders, submissions, and earnings.
  • To process payouts and maintain financial records.
  • To communicate order status, updates, delivery schedules, and support messages.
  • To prevent fraudulent activities or misuse of the app.
  • To improve app performance, analytics, and user experience.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information only in these cases:

  • With logistics partners: for delivering raw materials or picking up finished products.
  • With service providers: payment processors, cloud hosting, analytics services.
  • With legal authorities: when required by law or to protect users and the platform.
  • Business transfers: in case of merger, acquisition, or restructuring, under confidentiality agreements.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Storage & Security

We store your data securely using industry‑standard practices. While we take strong steps to protect your data, no system is fully secure. Data is retained only as long as necessary for business, financial, or legal purposes.

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your stored information.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal requirements).
  • Opt‑out of marketing messages.
  • Withdraw app permissions such as location access.

To exercise these rights, email us at the contact below. Identity verification may be required.

6. Children

OnlinePaisa is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover such data, we will remove it.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies, mobile identifiers, and analytics tools to improve functionality and measure performance. You can control these through device settings where applicable.

8. Third‑Party Integrations

The app may include payment gateways, map services, or identity verification tools. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries outside your own. We follow required legal standards to protect your data during such transfers.
